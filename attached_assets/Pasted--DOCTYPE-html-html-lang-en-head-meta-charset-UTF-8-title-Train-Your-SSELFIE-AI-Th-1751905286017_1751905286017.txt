    <h1 class="headline">The Only "Selfie Rules" You'll Ever Need</h1>
    <div class="intro">
      The better your SSELFIE AI knows you, the more editorial (and real) your images will be. Here's how to train your model so it captures every side, every mood, and your full story.
    </div>

    <div class="sandra-quote">
      "I went from hiding from cameras to building a 120K-follower brand. These are the exact selfie strategies that changed everything."
    </div>

    <ul class="editorial-list">
      <li>
        <strong>1. Face the window.</strong> Natural light flatters everyone. Stand in front of a window. No ring light, no studio. Just you and the sun doing their thing.
      </li>
      <li>
        <strong>2. Move around.</strong> Don't just smile and freeze. Turn left. Turn right. Look up, look down. Give us your profile. Your jawline, your wild side, your "I mean business" stare.
      </li>
      <li>
        <strong>3. Full body, half body, closeup.</strong> Give the AI range. One shot sitting, one standing, one just your face. Wear what you'd actually wear to meet your dream client.
      </li>
      <li>
        <strong>4. Don't chase "pretty."</strong> Laugh. Frown. Look serious. Messy bun, lipstick, no makeup. Show the real you. The model loves honesty more than perfection.
      </li>
      <li>
        <strong>5. Change locations.</strong> Try the kitchen, the couch, outside, in your car. Different backgrounds = more flexibility for your brand.
      </li>
    </ul>

      <div class="do-section">
        <div class="section-title">DO THIS</div>
        <ul class="tips-list">
          <li>Upload 10-15 different selfies</li>
          <li>Include various lighting conditions</li>
          <li>Show different emotions and expressions</li>
          <li>Mix up your outfits and accessories</li>
          <li>Include some with and without makeup</li>
          <li>Take photos from multiple angles</li>
          <li>Use your phone's portrait mode if available</li>
          <li>Show your hands and body, not just face</li>
        </ul>

      <div class="dont-section">
        <div class="section-title">AVOID THIS</div>
        <ul class="tips-list">
          <li>Heavy filters or editing</li>

          <li>Group photos (AI gets confused)</li>
          <li>Photos where your face is blocked</li>
          <li>Super dark or blurry images</li>
          <li>Screenshots from video calls</li>
          <li>Photos taken in a mirror</li>
          <li>Images with text overlays</li>
