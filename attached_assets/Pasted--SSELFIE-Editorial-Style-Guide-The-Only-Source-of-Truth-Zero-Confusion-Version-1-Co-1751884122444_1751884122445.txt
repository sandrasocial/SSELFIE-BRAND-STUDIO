# SSELFIE Editorial Style Guide  
*(The Only Source of Truth—Zero Confusion Version)*

---

## 1. Color Palette

| Name            | HEX         | Usage                                  |
|-----------------|-------------|----------------------------------------|
| Black           | #0a0a0a     | Headlines, backgrounds, CTA, nav       |
| White           | #ffffff     | Background, text, negative space       |
| Editorial Gray  | #f5f5f5     | Moodboard, testimonials, section BG    |
| Mid Gray        | #fafafa     | Default page BG, footer, nav BG        |
| Soft Gray       | #666666     | Subtext, muted copy, secondary UI      |
| Accent Line     | #e5e5e5     | Borders, separators, field outlines    |

**Strict rules:**  
- Only use colors listed here.  
- Never use color for “fun,” “emphasis,” or “decoration.”  
- No gradients, no pastel pinks or purples, no blue. Ever.

---

## 2. Typography

**There is only ONE headline font. No exceptions.**

- **Headlines / Editorial Titles:**  
  `font-family: 'Times New Roman', Times, serif;`  
  *Weight:* 200–400  
  *Case:* Sentence or Title  
  *Letter-spacing:* -0.01em (tight, luxe)  
  *No Bodoni, No Playfair, No substitutes. Ever.*

- **Body Copy & General UI:**  
  `font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Segoe UI', sans-serif;`  
  *Weight:* 300–400  
  *Case:* Sentence  
  *Letter-spacing:* normal

- **Subtext / Eyebrow / Stats / UI Labels:**  
  *Font:* Same as body  
  *Size:* 11px  
  *Letter-spacing:* 0.4em  
  *Case:* UPPERCASE  
  *Color:* #666666 (on light), rgba(255,255,255,0.5) (on black)  

**Don’t:**  
- Use Bodoni, Playfair, Georgia, or any other serif.  
- Use italics, drop shadows, or outline text.

---

## 3. Spacing & Layout

- **Section Padding:**  
  Desktop: 80–120px top/bottom  
  Mobile: 30–40px top/bottom
- **Grid Gaps:**  
  8–60px depending on context (err on more space)
- **Max Widths:**  
  Editorial: 1200px  
  Moodboard: 1400px  
  CTA: 700–900px
- **If it feels crowded, add more space. Luxury = air.**

---

## 4. Buttons & CTAs

- **Style:**  
  - Minimal, no fills or gradients  
  - All caps, 11–13px, tracked (0.3–0.4em letter-spacing)  
  - Underline or border on hover only  
  - No icons, no box-shadows, no rounded corners  
  - Use only #0a0a0a (black), #fff (white), or #e5e5e5 (accent)  
- **Copy:**  
  - Direct, never cutesy:  
    - BEGIN  
    - START YOUR JOURNEY  
    - SSELFIE METHOD  
    - 1:1 COACHING  
    - SEE MORE STORIES  

---

## 5. Imagery

- **Style:**  
  - Editorial, high contrast, slightly desaturated or true color  
  - No oversaturated, no Instagram “filters”  
  - Black & white for drama, color for energy  
  - Never random, “cute,” or clip art  
- **Layout:**  
  - Full-bleed or strict grid/collage  
  - Never border-radius, never floating, always intentional  
  - Plenty of negative space

---

## 6. UI Components

- **All UI elements (checkbox, input, alert, card, etc):**
  - Sharp corners only—no border-radius  
  - Borders: #e5e5e5, 1px, minimal  
  - No icons, no default browser blue, no shadows  
  - Text: system sans-serif, correct weights, UPPERCASE for labels only  
  - Hover/focus: underline or border only—no color fills

---

## 7. Copywriting & Voice

- **Every word = advice from your smartest friend**  
- Never corporate, never “boss babe,” never empowerment speak  
- Icelandic directness + warmth + “I’ve actually lived this” energy  
- Copy always passes these:
  - Would Sandra say it to a friend over coffee?
  - Would it make a tired mom feel seen—not shamed?
  - Does it sound lived-in, not “inspirational quote”?
- **No exclamation marks unless you’d actually shout it.**

---

## 8. Editorial Don’ts

- No Bodoni, Playfair, or any other “magazine” serif  
- No icons, emojis, illustrations, or clip art  
- No pastel or “fun” color  
- No gradients, animated backgrounds, or moving elements  
- No empowerment jargon, fake urgency, or marketing speak  
- No “cards” with rounded corners, no drop shadows, no glassmorphism

---

## 9. Section Structure

- **Hero:** Editorial headline (Times New Roman, tight), subtext/eyebrow, minimal CTA  
- **Editorial Spread:** Power quote, split image/story, magazine columns, stats, testimonials  
- **Moodboard:** Grid/collage, negative space, one strong quote  
- **CTA:** Minimal, all-caps, feels like an invitation

---

## 10. If You’re Ever Unsure…

Ask: “What would Sandra text her best friend at midnight?”  
That’s your answer. If you’re still not sure, default to LESS, not more.
