Your Visibility Strategy

How positioned experts get noticed consistently

You have your positioning framework. Now what?

This is where most professionals stall. They know what makes them different, but they don't know how to communicate it consistently without feeling like they're bragging or being pushy.

Here's the truth: visibility isn't about volume. It's about strategic consistency.

Recognized experts don't post constantly or attend every networking event. They show up strategically in the places where their specific expertise adds the most value.

The Strategic Visibility Plan

Choose Your Primary Channel

Where does your target audience already consume professional content? LinkedIn? Industry publications? Podcasts? Pick one and own it.

Develop Your Content Pillars

Three types of content that reinforce your positioning: Problem insights, Method explanations, and Transformation stories.

Create Your Consistency System

A sustainable schedule that you can maintain long-term. Better to post quality content weekly than to burn out trying to post daily.

The 90-Day Visibility Challenge

Commit to sharing your expertise consistently for 90 days. Not promotional content - valuable insights that demonstrate your unique perspective.

Track engagement, but more importantly, track the quality of conversations and opportunities that start finding you.

Remember: you're not trying to be famous. You're trying to be findable by the right people when they need your specific expertise.

Your expertise deserves to be seen. Now you have the framework to make it happen strategically and authentically.
